BY-LAW NO. 14648
A By-law to amend the Street and Traffic By-law No. 2849
regarding owner or occupier allowing deposit of refuse on street
THE COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF VANCOUVER, in public meeting, enacts as follows:
1. This by-law amends the indicated provisions of the Street and Traffic By-law No. 2849.
2. In section 84, Council adds a new subsection (3) in the correct numerical order as follows:
“(3) No owner or occupier of real property shall cause, permit or allow any rubbish,
sweepings, leaves, construction or demolition debris, paper, handbills, refuse or
other discarded materials or things to be removed from the real property and
deposited on a street or other public place.”.
3. This by-law is to come into force and take effect on the date of its enactment.
ENACTED by Council this 31st day of March, 2026
