BY-LAW NO. 14693
A By-law to amend
Vancouver Utilities Development Cost Levy By-law No. 12183
regarding miscellaneous amendments
THE COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF VANCOUVER, in public meeting, enacts as follows:
1. This by-law amends the indicated provisions or schedules of Vancouver Utilities Development
Cost Levy By-law No. 12183.
2. In section 1.2, Council adds a new definition in the correct alphabetical order as follows:
““Temporary Accommodation for Medical Care” means the use of premises on the land zoned
as CD-1 (926) By-law No. 14691 to provide temporary accommodation with associated on-
site services at below-market rates for children or other individuals seeking medical care at
local health facilities, and their families and caregivers, but excludes a Community Care or
Assisted Living Facility or Group Residence;”.
3. In Schedule “C”, Council strikes out the bottom table and substitutes a new table as follows:
