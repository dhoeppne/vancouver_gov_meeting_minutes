BY-LAW NO. 14690
A By-law to amend the Ticket Offences By-law No. 9360
regarding miscellaneous amendments
THE COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF VANCOUVER, in public meeting, enacts as follows:
1. This by-law amends the indicated provisions and schedules of the Ticket Offences
By-law No. 9360.
2. Council inserts “building inspector,” before “property use inspector” in the definition of
Chief Building Official in section 1.2.
3. Council adds “Chief Building Official” to Column 1 of Table 4 of the By-law.
4. This by-law is to come into force and take effect on the date of its enactment.
ENACTED by Council this 5th day of May, 2026
Signed “Ken Sim”
Mayor
Signed “Katrina Leckovic”
City Clerk
