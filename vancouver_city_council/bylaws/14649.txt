BY-LAW NO. 14649
A By-law to amend the Ticket Offences By-law No. 9360
regarding the Street and Traffic By-law and the Solid Waste By-law
THE COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF VANCOUVER, in public meeting, enacts as follows:
1. This By-law amends the indicated provisions and schedules of the Ticket Offences
By-law.
2. In Table 5.2, Council:
(a) in Column 4 for sections 84(1) and 84(2), strikes out “$250.00” and substitutes
“$500.00”; and
(b) adds a new row of entries in Columns 2, 3 and 4 in the correct numerical order as
follows:
“
Owner or occupier allows deposit of refuse on street s. 84(3) $500.00
”.
3. In Column 3 of Table 9, Council strikes out “s. 5.9”, “s. 5.12(1)”, “s. 5.12(2)”, “s. 5.13(1)”,
“s. 5.13(2)”, “s. 5.14(1)” and “s. 5.14(2)” and substitutes “s. 5.3”, “s. 5.6(1)”, “s. 5.6(2)”, “s. 5.7(1)”,
“s. 5.7 (2)”, “s. 5.8(1)” and “s. 5.8(2)”, respectively.
4. A decision by a court that any part of this by-law is illegal, void, or unenforceable severs
that part from this by-law, and is not to affect the balance of this by-law.
5. This by-law is to come into force and take effect on the date of its enactment, except that
section 3 is to come into force and effect on January 1, 2026.
ENACTED by Council this 31st day of March, 2026
Signed “Ken Sim”
Mayor
Signed “Katrina Leckovic”
City Clerk
