BY-LAW NO. 14662
A By-law to repeal the
Greenhouse Gas Emission Reduction Official Development Plan
By-law No. 10041
WHEREAS:
Council has adopted the Vancouver Official Development Plan, which includes greenhouse gas
emission reduction targets, policies and actions, as required by Section 562.04(3) of the
Vancouver Charter.
THE COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF VANCOUVER, in public meeting, enacts as follows:
1. This by-law repeals the Greenhouse Gas Emission Reduction Official Development, as
amended, that was originally adopted by By-law No. 10041.
2. This by-law is to come into force and take effect on the date of its enactment.
ENACTED by Council this 31st day of March, 2026

Mayor
