BY-LAW NO. 14661
A By-law to repeal the
Regional Context Statement Official Development Plan
By-law No. 10789
WHEREAS:
Council has adopted an updated Regional Context Statement as part of the Vancouver Official
Development Plan.
THE COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF VANCOUVER, in public meeting, enacts as follows:
1. This by-law repeals the Regional Context Statement Official Development Plan, as
amended, that was originally adopted by By-law No. 10789.
2. This by-law is to come into force and take effect on the date of its enactment.
ENACTED by Council this 31st day of March, 2026
Signed “Ken Sim”
Mayor
Signed “Katrina Leckovic”
City Clerk
