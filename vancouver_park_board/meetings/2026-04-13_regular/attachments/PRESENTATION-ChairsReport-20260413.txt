CHAIR’S REPORT
Regular Park Board Meeting
Monday, April 13, 2026Liaison Roles – Chair Digby
In role as PB liaison, Chair Digby attended meetings with the Association Presidents Group, the Vancouver City
Planning Commission, Britannia Community Centre Association, and met with members of the Dr. Sun Yat-Sen
Classical Chinese Garden Society.Season Opener at Trout Lake Little League
Chair Digby attended the opening of the Trout Lake Little League season with Minister Dix and former Park Board
Chair Aaron JasperPickleball at Jericho
Chair Digby learned to play pickleball at the Jericho courts, seen here with Janet Martini, President of the
Vancouver Pickleball Association and their court ambassador Lawrence CarlsonWPG Lawn Bowling Club Tour
Chair Digby attended a tour of the West Point Grey Lawn Bowling Club at Trimble Park and congratulated the
President Heather Friesen on a successful member fundraised to re-shingle the leaky roof.Chair Meetings
Chair Digby communicated the Board’s Think 50 Metre motion requesting funding to all local Federal MPs
and MLAs, and had follow up meetings about the funding with Minister Robertson, MP Davies, and MLA
Dix. He and Commissioner Jensen also met with Cllr. Bligh to discuss the 2027-2030 Capital Plan motion
passed by the Board on April 7Moberly Park Visit
Commissioner Haer and Commissioner Virdi, alongside Mayor Ken Sim and City Councillors, visited the
site for the new turf field at Moberly Park.Park Visits – Comr. Jensen
Since the last Park Board meeting, Commissioner Jensen has visited Jonathan Rogers Park community garden,
and Granville Park East and West to enjoy the new dog park and playground. He also checked out the renovation
at the washrooms.Park Visits – Comr. Bastyovanszky
Since the last Park Board meeting, Commissioner Bastyovanszky (along with Commissioner Jensen)
visited Nat Bailey Stadium and Ross ParkLunar New Year
Commissioner Haer attended Lunar New Year celebrations alongside City Councillors, welcoming in the
Year of the Fire Horse.Italian Canadian Sports Federation Gala
Commissioner Haer attended the Italian Canadian Sports Federation and Columbus FC Gala and Awards
Night, alongside Councillors Dominato and Kirby-Yung and former Columbus FC player Joe Cuzzetto.Remembering Dr. Setty Pendakur
Commissioner Haer attended a memorial for Dr. Setty Pendakur. Dr. Pendakur was an urban planner,
educator, and civic leader whose work helped shape Vancouver and contributed meaningfully to
communities across Canada and internationally.Equip Sport Launch
Commissioner Virdi attended the media announcement of the Equip Sport free sports equipment lending
stations.Harold Johnson Memorial
Commissioner Haer attended a recent memorial for Harold Johnson. Harold was a pillar of the community
who showed up day after day, supporting seniors, checking in on local businesses, and helping ensure that
people felt safe and connected.Community Dinner of Remembrance
Commissioner Haer attended the Community Dinner of Remembrance alongside Mayor Sim, City
Councillors and School Trustees. This gathering was an opportunity to stand with the Filipino community
following the tragic events of Lapu-Lapu Day.Lapu-Lapu Day Memorial Mass
Commissioner Virdi attended memorial mass at St. Andrews Church, held for victims of the Lapu-Lapu Day
tragedy2026 State of the City
Commissioner Haer attended Mayor Sim’s 2026 State of the City address, alongside City Councillors,
elected officials, dignitaries, and members of the business community.Sakura Days Japan Fair
Chair Digby attended the Sakura Days Japan Fair at VanDusen Botanical Garden, seen here with Vancouver
Cherry Blossom Festival director Linda Poole.Vaisakhi Parade 2026
Commissioners and City Councillors attended Vaisakhi at the Khalsa Diwan Temple on Ross Street, and
the Vaisakhi Parade and celebrations along the route.
