Final motion as amended
THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED
A. THAT the Vancouver Park Board approve the proposed concept plan for
the Moberly Park amenities including a new synthetic turf field, a new fieldhouse and
a new walking loop as described in this report.
B. THAT the Vancouver Park Board approve the proposed concept plan for the Ross
Park amenities including a new spray pad, a new washroom building and playground
renewal as described in this report; and
C. THAT the proposed development will incorporate the provision for a future small
meeting/community room, to be implemented when funding becomes available, and
situated adjacent to the washroom facilities at Ross Park, without impacting the current
design timeline. This approach will preserve the existing amenity while enabling the
creation of a safe, enclosed, and weather-protected environment to support year-
round social activities.
