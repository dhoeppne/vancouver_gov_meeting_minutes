8Recommendations
A. THAT the Vancouver Park Board endorse the concept of changes to Harbour Green Dock to
enable a commercial electric foot passenger ferry service to operate at the site while continuing
to provide a public amenity for recreational watercraft moorage and a public viewing platform;
B. THAT the Vancouver Park Board indicate their support for City staff to negotiate, to the
satisfaction of the City Engineer and the City’s Director of Legal Services, and in consultation
with the Park Board’s General Manager, and enter into an agreement with CIRQL
Ferries Incorporated to deliver the services and amenities described above, subject to the
agreement being within the parameters outlined in this report;
C. THAT, subject to the execution of the agreement described in Recommendation B, the
Vancouver Park Board direct staff to prepare a Memorandum of Understanding with City
departments confirming that ownership, operation, and maintenance responsibilities for Harbour
Green Dock and supporting infrastructure shall henceforth be held by Engineering Services,
