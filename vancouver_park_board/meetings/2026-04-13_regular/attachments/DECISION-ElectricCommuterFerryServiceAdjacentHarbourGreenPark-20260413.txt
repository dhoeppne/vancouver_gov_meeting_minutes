Electric Commuter Ferry Service Adjacent Harbour Green Park
The below is for information only. Minutes contain the official record of Board decisions.

Final motion as amended
THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED
A. THAT the Vancouver Park Board endorse the concept of changes to Harbour Green
Dock to enable a commercial electric foot passenger ferry service to operate at the
site while continuing to provide a public amenity for recreational watercraft moorage
and a public viewing platform;
B. THAT the Vancouver Park Board indicate their support for City staff to negotiate, to
the satisfaction of the City Engineer and the City’s Director of Legal Services, and in
consultation with the Park Board’s General Manager, and enter into an agreement with
CIRQL Ferries Incorporated to deliver the services and amenities described above,
subject to the agreement being within the parameters outlined in this report;
C. THAT, subject to the execution of the agreement described in Recommendation B, the
Vancouver Park Board direct staff to prepare a Memorandum of Understanding with
City departments confirming that ownership, operation, and maintenance
responsibilities for Harbour Green Dock and supporting infrastructure shall henceforth
be held by Engineering Services, reflecting the transportation-primary focus of the
amenity and the City’s water lot tenure; and
FURTHER THAT staff request negotiations for an appropriate revenue share with city
engineering to share with Park Board the revenue the city receives from the service,
ideally 50% of any city proceeds (after costs);
