▪ Public engagement events: Two rounds of events in

8Finance
▪ The estimated capital cost for Burrard Slopes Park is
$13.1M, based on a ‘Class D’ construction estimate including
soft costs, project management fees, permitting, and
contingencies.
▪ $8.8M is anticipated to be funded through 2027-2030 Capital
Plan, remainder funded from previous capital plan.
▪ The initial estimate for the operating impact to capital budget
is 5% of the estimated project construction cost or $400K per
year, starting in 2031.Timeline and Next Steps
▪ Following soil remediation, the site will be fenced and seeded with a
temporary meadow, which will be in place until construction begins.
▪ Construction will be phased, with the majority of the park done in
Phase 1, and the new parcel and associated areas completed in
Phase 2.
