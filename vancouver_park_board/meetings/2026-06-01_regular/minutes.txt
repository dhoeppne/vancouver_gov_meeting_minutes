STAFF REPORTS
1. Bard on the Beach – New Multi-Year Agreement Framework for Main Festival Site at
Vanier Park
THAT the Board Committee recommend to the Board:
A. THAT the Vancouver Board of Parks and Recreation (“Park Board”) direct staff to
explore the development of a new multi-year agreement framework for Bard on the
Beach at their main festival location in Vanier Park;
B. THAT, subject to a positive assessment, the Park Board direct staff to present the
proposed terms of a new agreement framework to the Board at the earliest
possible date; and
C. THAT no legal rights or obligations shall arise, and no consents, permissions, or
licenses are granted hereby, and none shall arise or be granted hereafter unless
and until all contemplated legal documentation has been executed and delivered
by all parties.
ADOPTED ON CONSENT
