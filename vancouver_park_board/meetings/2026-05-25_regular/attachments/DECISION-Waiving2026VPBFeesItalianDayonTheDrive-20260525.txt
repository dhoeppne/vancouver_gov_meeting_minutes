Waiving 2026 Vancouver Park Board Fees for Italian Day on The Drive
The below is for information only. Minutes contain the official record of Board decisions.
~ ~ ~ ~ ~
Final motion
THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED THAT The Vancouver Board of Parks and Recreation
direct staff to waive all applicable Vancouver Park Board fees associated with the 2026
Italian Day on The Drive festival, including event-related permit and park use fees where
operationally feasible.
Park Board Meeting – May 25, 2026
