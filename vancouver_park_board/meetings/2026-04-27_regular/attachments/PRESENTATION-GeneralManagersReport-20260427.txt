Monday, April 27, 2026Memorial West Park Improvements
▪ The irrigation system installed in 1980 had reached the end
of life and was no longer operational
▪ Complete irritation system replacement has now been
completed
▪ 4,800 linear feet of irrigation piping, wiring, control boxes
and irrigation heads were installed
▪ Key Benefits of the New Irrigation System:
▪ Targeted water delivery improves efficiency and delivers
significant water use savings
▪ Improved field conditions with fewer dry spots and reduced
water pooling
▪ Remote system control capability enables additional
operational and water savings
▪ Simplified maintenance and troubleshooting, reducing labour
and response time
▪ Enhanced safety, playability, consistency, and overall field
