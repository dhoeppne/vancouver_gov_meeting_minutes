2026 CITY OF VANCOUVER
Public Fireworks Celebration -
Special Event
Park Board Committee Meeting
Monday, April 27, 2026PURPOSE/OBJECTIVES
▪ Present the 2026 City of
Vancouver (COV) Public
Fireworks Celebration to the
Board
▪ Seek Board approval for this
application
▪ Authorize the General Manager
of the Park Board to approve the
detailed event terms/conditions
2BACKGROUND
▪ Indefinite cancellation of the
Celebration of Light (COL)
fireworks festival announced in
November 2025
▪ Vancouver’s Mayor publicly
expressed disappointment over
reductions in senior-government
funding; affirmed the City’s
willingness to explore ways to
ensure a major summer fireworks
celebration takes place
3BACKGROUND
▪ City Council approved a motion in
February 2026, directing staff to
plan and deliver a one-night public
fireworks event in Summer 2026
▪ Staff have initiated discussions
and preliminary preparations for a
one-night public fireworks event
▪ Staff are working to finalize event
date and operational details
4PROPOSAL
▪ COV is seeking approval to
deliver a free and accessible one-
night fireworks event at English
Bay Beach Park in Summer 2026
(date to be determined)
▪ More detailed event planning still
required
▪ Initiative is expected to follow a
delivery model similar to COL,
albeit with less features and
complexity
5PROPOSAL
▪ Event will feature a modest
lounge space with alcohol service
on the bathhouse rooftop and a
small selection of food trucks
▪ Event will not include:
• Drone show
• The Inukshuk Lounge hosting space
• Large grandstand seating
• Activations in Morton Park and
adjacent spaces
6PROPOSAL
▪ Event production will be
outsourced to qualified team with
demonstrated experience
▪ NOITC issued signaling intent to
engage BrandLive Group as their
production partner
▪ Park Board will act as an event
facilitator
▪ Event to be supported by FEST
Committee and FWC26 Host
Committee
7PRELIMINARY SITE PLAN
8BENEFITS
▪ Large scale engagement of
residents and visitors
▪ Building community connections
and civic pride
▪ Collaboration and support for local
suppliers and partners
▪ Promotion of Vancouver as a
destination for tourism, support for
long-term economic activity, and
enhancement of the City’s brand
9KEY CONSIDERATIONS
▪ Financial
• All expenses to be borne by the event
organizer; no costs to Park Board
▪ Beach/park access
• Organizer will develop plan to minimize
disruptions to public access
• Set-up/tear-down timeline (4 days)
▪ Neighbourhood notification
• Organizer will prepare and deliver a
communication strategy/plan
10SUMMARY
▪ 2026 City of Vancouver Public
Fireworks Celebration presents an
exciting opportunity to deliver a
popular fireworks event to Vancouver
residents and visitors
▪ Free and fully accessible event will
showcase the City’s premier
waterfront spaces and contribute to
the City’s cultural, tourism, and
economic vibrancy
▪ Staff have considered the many
aspects of this proposal; confident it
can be implemented successfully
11RECOMMENDATIONS
A. THAT the Vancouver Park Board approve an application from the City of
Vancouver to host the 2026 Public Fireworks Celebration at English Bay
Beach Park with all arrangements to the satisfaction of the General
Manager of the Park Board.
B. THAT the Park Board approve an application from the City of Vancouver for
a Special Event Permit from the BC Liquor & Cannabis Regulation Branch
to serve alcohol at the aforementioned location with a maximum capacity of
100 guests.
