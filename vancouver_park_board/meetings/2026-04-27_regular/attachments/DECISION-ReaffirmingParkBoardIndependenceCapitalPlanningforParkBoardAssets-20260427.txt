Final motion
THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED
A. THAT the Board directs staff to continue with the Capital Plan process without regard
to the projects listed within the Council motion of April 22, 2026, and prioritize projects
based on already approved Park board plans, strategies, and previous Board
directions;
B. THAT the Board retain independent legal counsel to provide advice on the jurisdiction
of Park Board granted under the Vancouver Charter to make Capital Plan allocations
in public parks, and to advise on the scope of City Council authority in this regard, if
any; and
FURTHER THAT costs for such legal counsel be drawn from the remaining $20,000
allocation made by the Board at the January 22, 2024 meeting (Independent Legal
Advice for Judicial Review of Mayor’s Motion).
