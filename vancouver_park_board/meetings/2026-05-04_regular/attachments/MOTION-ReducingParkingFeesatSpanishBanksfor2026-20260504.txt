MOTION on NOTICE
Submitted at the Regular Park Board Meeting on April 27, 2026
Reducing Parking Fees at Spanish Banks for 2026
SUBMITTED BY: Commissioner Digby
WHEREAS
1. Historically, car parking rates at Spanish Banks were free up to 2023, $1.00/h in 2024 and
$1.50/h 2025;
At the Special Meeting on Nov 24, 2025, Commissioners approved the maximum
allowable parking fee for Spanish Banks to be $4.25/h, with the condition that the Board
delegate authority to the General Manager of Parks and Recreation to temporarily adjust
fees within the 2026 fiscal year to respond to market conditions, and to not exceed the
maximum allowable fee (See Minutes of Meeting of November 24, 2025)
2. Since April 1, 2026, the parking rate at Spanish Banks has been $4.25/h;
3. The city charged parking rate in the city owned lot at nearby Locarno beach is currently
$1.50/h.
THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED
A. THAT The Vancouver Park Board hereby directs staff to immediately change the car
parking rates at Spanish Banks to $3.00/h or lower for the summer period of 2026;
B. FURTHER THAT the pricing at Spanish Banks bear a closer relation to the city charged
parking rates at Locarno Beach; and
C. FURTHER THAT the Chair be directed to send a letter to Evo Car Share and its owner
BCCA, encouraging them to include Spanish Banks sin its home zone for car drops.
Park Board Meeting: May 4, 2026
