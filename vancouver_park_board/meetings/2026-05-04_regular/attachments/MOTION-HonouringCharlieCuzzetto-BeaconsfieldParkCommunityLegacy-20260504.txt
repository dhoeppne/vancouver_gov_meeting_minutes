MOTION on NOTICE
Submitted at the Regular Park Board Meeting on April 27, 2026
Honouring Charlie Cuzzetto – Beaconsfield Park Community Legacy
SUBMITTED BY: Commissioner Haer
WHEREAS
1. Charlie Cuzzetto has made significant contributions to the Vancouver community through
over four decades of service within the parks and recreation system and more than 50
years of volunteerism; and
2. Mr. Cuzzetto has played a meaningful role in advancing community-driven improvements
at Beaconsfield Park; and
3. He is currently facing significant health challenges, making it both timely and appropriate
to recognize his contributions during his lifetime;
THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED
A. THAT The Vancouver Park Board formally acknowledge and honour the contributions of
Charlie Cuzzetto to Beaconsfield Park and the broader Vancouver parks system;
B. THAT staff be directed to report back with options to commemorate Mr. Cuzzetto’s legacy
within the Beaconsfield Park project, including:
i. Consideration of naming the new turf field at Beaconsfield Park in his honour,
consistent with Park Board naming policies;
ii. Other appropriate recognition measures, including plaques, dedications, or
naming of park features;
C. Staff report back in a timely manner to enable the Board to consider and implement
recognition without delay.
Park Board Meeting: May 4, 2026
