Final motion as amended
THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED
A. THAT the Vancouver Park Board approve the proposed concept plan for the
Beaconsfield Park – Synthetic Turf Field as described in this report and shown
in Appendix A with the condition that the field be designed to achieve a minimum
playable width of 60 metres, and ideally 64 metres;
B. THAT staff continue to work with interested parties and partner groups through
detailed design to confirm project details, cost-sharing or private contributions;
and
C. THAT the Board direct staff to share this report with Mayor & Council, for
information, in relation the Council motion approved on September 17, 2025,
titled “Accelerating the Turf Field Upgrade at Beaconsfield Park for Inclusion in
the 2027-2030 Capital Plan”
Park Board Meeting – March 30, 2026
