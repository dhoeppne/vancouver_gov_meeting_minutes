5Financial Considerations
Total estimated project costs for Columbia Park Concept Phase 1 is $19M, to be cost
shared with Engineering.
Phase 1 Funding:
▪ Engineering Contribution: Engineering is offering up to a 50% cost share for this first phase
of park renewal to compensate for the use of parkland and replacement of existing impacted
features, for an underground stormwater storage tank.
▪ Park Board Contribution: Requires a funding allocation of approx. $9.7M in the 2027 to
2030 Capital Plan to advance detailed design and construction, pending Board decision.
▪ Estimated annual operating impact to the capital budget is ~$0.44M, including $0.06M for
synthetic turf operations
6Synthetic Turf vs Natural Turf Field Key Considerations
▪ Challenge of comparing costs: Capital cost alone is
misleading because while syn. turf has a higher upfront cost, it
supports significantly higher use, which directly influences
revenue potential and cost recovery.
▪ High Density Area: Effective field closures for rest and
recovery of natural turf fields is difficult in high-density areas.
Limiting informal use requires a level of enforcement that is not
feasible, and fencing is not recommended due to unintended
uses (ie dogs)
▪ Park Provision: As synthetic turf fields accommodate more
use at a single field, there is an opportunity to free up scarce,
valuable space in the park system for other park uses.
